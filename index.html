Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the questions that slow down solar decisions

Is this website only for homes, or also for businesses?

Your Energy is built for both. The free assessment flow handles residential and commercial leads through one cleaner experience.

Can I get a rough estimate before a site visit?

Yes. The free assessment provides a directional estimate based on your bill, property type, and location. A full quote becomes more accurate after an assessment.

Do you support on-grid, off-grid, and hybrid solar?

Yes. The website is designed to explain each solution path clearly so the recommendation can match the property’s real needs.

What happens after the installation is complete?

Your Energy also positions maintenance, AMC support, and long-term system care as part of the customer journey.

Do you help with subsidy-related guidance?

Yes. The team can help explain the process and what is usually required, while final rules and approval depend on the current applicable guidelines.
